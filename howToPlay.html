How to play

Match the digits on your tickets with the winning numbers in the correct order to win a share of the prize pool. It’s that simple!

1

Visit the Finichain Website

First, visit the Finichain website and create an account. You can sign up using two methods:

Sign up with email and password: Simply enter your email and set a password to create your account. Connect a wallet: You can easily connect your crypto wallet and log in instantly. Finichain supports popular wallets like MetaMask and Trust Wallet.

2

Select a Ticket

After connecting your wallet or signing in with your email, go to the main game page. You can purchase lottery tickets for $2 (in any cryptocurrency of your choice).

3

Convert to RABI

After purchasing a ticket, the amount you deposited will automatically be converted to RABI tokens. This process happens automatically, so you don’t need to worry about it. If you pay using RABI tokens, you'll be eligible for special rewards.

4

Choose a Lottery Period

First, visit the Finichain website and create an account. You can sign up using two methods:

1 Month: You'll participate in the monthly lottery, with a chance to win instant prizes. 3 Months: By choosing this option, you'll enter the three-month lottery with bigger rewards. 1 Year (Grand Prize): The biggest prize is for the yearly lottery. You can purchase a ticket to get a chance to win the grand prize after one year.

5

Select Your Lottery Code

After funding your account and choosing the desired lottery period, you'll proceed to the ticket selection. At this stage, a 6-digit code will be generated. You can choose the 6 digits in two ways:

Manual selection: Choose 6 numbers between 1 and 9. Automatic selection: Let the system generate a random 6-digit code for you. This 6-digit number will be your lottery code. After confirming your choice (whether manual or automatic), your ticket will be registered, and you'll participate in the selected lottery.

6

Wait for the Results

After purchasing a ticket, the amount you deposited will automatically be converted to RABI tokens. This process happens automatically, so you don’t need to worry about it. If you pay using RABI tokens, you'll be eligible for special rewards.

7

Receiving Prizes

If you signed up using an email and password, you will need to register your wallet in the system so that your prize can be automatically sent to your wallet if you win. If you signed up by connecting your wallet, your prize will automatically be sent to the connected wallet. In case of any issues, don't worry—our support team will assist you through the ticket system.
