Join the Finichain lottery by purchasing a ticket for just $2 (payable in any cryptocurrency) and get a chance to win exciting prizes. Rewards are given in RABI tokens, with monthly, three-month, and a grand prize draw at the end of the year!

How to play

Match the digits on your tickets with the winning numbers in the correct order to win a share of the prize pool. It’s that simple!

STEP 1

Buy Tickets

Ticket prices are set at 2 USD, payable in any cryptocurrency (prizes are awarded in RABI). After depositing 2 USD, it will be converted into RABI.

STEP 2

Wait for the Draw

Based on your ticket, draws will occur over three periods: one month, three months, and a grand prize after one year.

STEP 3

Check for Prizes

Once the period ends, return to the site and check if you’ve won!

Terms

All participants must purchase tickets using any cryptocurrency, with the equivalent value converted to RABI. Each ticket grants entry into draws based on the selected period (one month, three months, or one year). Participants must ensure the accuracy of their wallet details when purchasing tickets. Prizes are distributed in RABI tokens and must be claimed within a specified time after the draw. Any fraudulent activity or attempt to manipulate the draw will result in disqualification and potential account suspension.

Prize Funds

The prize pool for each lottery round is distributed from the following sources :

Ticket Purchases

100% of the 2 USD paid in cryptocurrency by participants is allocated as follows: 60% goes to the winning ticket holder, 10% is donated to charity, and 30% is retained as company revenue.

Rollover Prizes

If no one wins in a specific prize bracket during a round, the unclaimed RABI from that bracket rolls over into the next round and is redistributed among the prize pools.

RABI Injections

Additional RABI tokens may be injected into the prize pool from time to time to boost rewards and add excitement to future rounds.
